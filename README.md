MISSION OF THE APP:
  To provide a centralized digital repository for the College of Computing and Multimedia Studies (CCMS), specifically for 
  IT and IS programs. CCMS Archive aims to eliminate the frustration of academic redundancy by offering a structured platform 
  for browsing past research, project demonstrations, and technical documentation. By making previous student outputs easily 
  accessible, the mission is to foster a culture of innovation, streamline the topic proposal process, and promote meaningful
  academic collaboration between current students and past project creators.

What can students do?
  1. View other projects.
  2. Add their own projects (must be approved).
  3. See the status of their project through the notifications.
  4. Bookmark projects.
  5. Message other projects.

What can instructors do?
  1. View Projects.
  2. Approve or reject projects through the app or email.
  3. Bookmark projects.
  4. Message students.
